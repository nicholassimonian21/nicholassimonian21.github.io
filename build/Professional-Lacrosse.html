The MLL vs. the PLL

Nicholas Simonian

11/5/20 at 11:00am

The MLL, or Major League Lacrosse, has been the main professional league for the game of lacrosse. It was the most popular, well-known, and elite league and was the home of many of the top professional lacrosse players for a while. However, the PLL, or Premier Lacrosse League, was enacted much more recently to be the replacement league. It has gained a lot of popularity because many of the top players switched over to this new league.

Although they seem fairly similar, the MLL and PLL have many differences between them. The way that drafts are conducted, the rules regarding teams, player counts, and the like are very different. The PLL has also garnered a lot of attention, so games are being sold out in cpomparison to the MLL.
